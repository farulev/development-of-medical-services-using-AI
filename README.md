## 🧩 Problem

Healthcare systems face systemic inefficiencies:

* 🧑‍⚕️ Shortage of qualified specialists
* ⏱️ Long diagnostic turnaround times
* 📉 Human error in high-load environments
* 🧠 Fragmented clinical knowledge

> In radiology alone, error rates can reach **3–5%**, with significantly higher rates under workload stress.

---

## 💡 Solution

A **regulatory-ready AI platform** that integrates into clinical workflows and provides:

* Automated medical image analysis (CT, MRI, X-ray)
* Clinical decision support
* Risk stratification and prioritization
* Continuous learning from new data (privacy-preserving)
